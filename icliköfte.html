In a random Restaurant

Sometimes is random a good thing..

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 1/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/3 cup walnut halves, or shelled pistachios, ground
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot red pepper flakes

For the case:

  • 1/3 cup fine bulgur
  • 1 tablespoon ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup boiled mashed potato
  • 1/2 large egg, beaten
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 3 to 4 cups sunflower oil, or other light oil for frying
  • Fresh Italian parsley, for garnish

icli köfte is a dish from the city of Gaziantep. Unfortunately I haven't been to Gaziantep yet, but this would be my next destination. Fortunately, however, I was able to find a good restaurant during my vacation where I could try Icli Kofte.

when i placed the order in the restaurant, i was pleased to find that the icli köftes were well portioned. One icli köfte will not fill you up, but 4-5 pieces should definitely fill you up. It's a challenge and you can turn it into an eating contest to eat as many icli koftes as you can. Cola goes particularly well with icli köfte.
