a trip to Nevsehir

A great place to live...

Ingredients:

For 2 Persons:

  • 1/2 kg chicken, lamb, or beef
  • 1 big onion, cut into cubes
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 green bell peppers, cut into cubes
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 head garlic
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Dried or fresh thyme for flavoring
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

In the further course of our vacation we are to Nevsehir. This is very far east of Turkey. It's a great place with a beautiful castle and a great history. One dish I can recommend is testi kebabi.
Since handmade clay work is very common in this city and it is also one of the symbols of the city, it is not surprising that this tool is also used in the preparation of food.
The name "Testi" is meaning jug which is just made by clay. this clay vessel is then sealed with dough and roasted in a stone oven. After a certain time, the vessel is then removed from the hot oven and, with a great show, the vessel is broken very precisely and professionally. What came out of it you can see very well in the picture above.

You can drink a cola or a fresh ayran. There are various salads and appetizers as side dishes. These are usually not on the bill in Turkey because these side dishes are free additions to the house.
